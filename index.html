About Us

Phaahla Mason Colossal Projects (Pty) Ltd is 100% black owned Professional Civil Engineering Building construction company with expertise in Civil Engineering services. The services includes Building Construction, Project Management, Architectural Design and and Refurbishment.

Our Values

   Vision

To maintain the highest levels of professionalism, integrity, honesty and fairness in our relationships with our suppliers, subcontractors, professional associates and customers.

  Mission

To grow as one of South Africa leading building Construction with a commitment to deliver excellence in execution, continues improvement, qualitative brilliance and mission-critical values to our clients. We want to become a successful a successful growing company not only in civil engineering only but other open opportunities in the market.

  Competitive

At PMC Projects, we strive for excellence, by ensuring that we are always up to par with industry-related practices, this way we stay competitive in our industry.
